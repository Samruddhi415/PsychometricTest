Understanding Your OCEAN Traits

Openness

Describes your appreciation for art, emotion, adventure, unusual ideas, curiosity, and variety of experience.

High Openness Traits:

  • Creative and imaginative
  • Curious about new ideas and experiences
  • Appreciates art, music, and culture
  • Enjoys abstract thinking

Conscientiousness

Reflects your tendency to be organized and dependable, show self-discipline, aim for achievement, and prefer planned rather than spontaneous behavior.

High Conscientiousness Traits:

  • Organized and structured
  • Attention to detail
  • Goal-oriented and persistent
  • Reliable and responsible

Extraversion

Characterized by energy, positive emotions, assertiveness, sociability and the tendency to seek stimulation in the company of others.

High Extraversion Traits:

  • Outgoing and sociable
  • Energized by social interaction
  • Comfortable in group settings
  • Enthusiastic and expressive

Agreeableness

Reflects your tendency to be compassionate and cooperative rather than suspicious and antagonistic towards others.

High Agreeableness Traits:

  • Empathetic and caring
  • Cooperative and helpful
  • Trusting of others
  • Conflict-averse

Neuroticism

Describes your tendency to experience unpleasant emotions easily, such as anger, anxiety, depression, and vulnerability.

High Neuroticism Traits:

  • Emotionally reactive
  • Prone to stress and worry
  • Self-conscious
  • Sensitive to criticism

Low Neuroticism (Emotional Stability) Traits:

  • Calm and resilient
  • Steady under pressure
  • Less easily upset

About This Analysis

This analysis is based on facial expressions captured during your session. Emotions displayed on your face were analyzed and mapped to the Big Five (OCEAN) personality traits. The chart above shows the relative strength of each trait in your personality profile.

Remember that personality is complex and can vary across different situations. This analysis provides a snapshot based on your expressions during this particular session.
